Game Info

In Fort Nitta, each player is in control of a set of castles. To achieve victory, players must attack other castles while defending their own. To defend against enemy attacks, players are able to rebuild castle walls.

At the start of battle each player must choose the location of their fort to strategically build their castle around. Players will then place cannons within the castle.

placeholder

Afterwards, the attack round will commence and each player will take turns shooting at other players using the respective cannons. Wind levels could also vary during the attack round, which will affect the accuracy of projectiles

Damages done during this round will be able to be repaired in the following repair round with a given time limit. Blocks given for repairs are similar to Tetris shapes, and can be moved freely around to build castle walls. Survivors of the repair round will receive additional cannons to continue the battle.
